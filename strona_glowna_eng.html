Caserta

Caserta - a restaurant that for generations has given you the opportunity to taste traditional Italian dishes in their best form - this is confirmed by the crowds of guests who constantly come to our restaurant.

Our restaurant offers takeout or home/office delivery orders.

In the summer season, we invite you to our garden, where you can taste Italian cuisine in the open air.

Caserta is a wonderful place to organize a special event. We are here for our guests, so our chefs are open to any menu proposals that we will serve at your event.

We cordially invite you!
