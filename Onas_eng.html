Caserta

Restaurant "Caserta" is a place where you can try authentic Italian dishes. It was founded in 2015 by the owner who was fascinated by the beauty of the city of Caserta and its rich history and culinary culture. The name of the restaurant refers to this city, and the dishes offered are inspired by traditional Italian recipes. Guests can feel like in a real Italian trattoria and taste flavors that will remind them of holidays in Italy.
